Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about SocialConnect

Yes, SocialConnect is completely free for all users. We believe authentic social connection shouldn't come with a price tag. Our core features will always remain free, with optional premium features planned for the future that enhance but don't replace the base experience.

Privacy is built into every aspect of SocialConnect. We use end-to-end encryption for messages, never sell your data to third parties, and give you granular control over who can see your content. We also undergo regular security audits and publish transparency reports.

Unlike platforms that prioritize engagement metrics, we focus on meaningful connections. There are no algorithmic feeds designed to maximize time spent, no data harvesting for advertising, and no artificial engagement boosting. We're building a platform that serves users, not advertisers.

We provide tools to help you find friends who are already on SocialConnect, but we don't automatically import contact lists. We believe in intentional connections, so we encourage users to invite friends individually and build their network thoughtfully.

We have a comprehensive moderation system that combines automated detection with human review. Our community guidelines are clear and consistently enforced. Users can report content easily, and we respond quickly to maintain a safe environment for all users.

Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are currently in development and planned for release in Q2 2025. The mobile apps will include all web features plus mobile-specific enhancements like push notifications and offline message sync.
