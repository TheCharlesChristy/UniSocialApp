Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

To create an account on SocialConnect, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your email, username, and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Use the search function to find people by name or username. You can also import contacts from your email or phone. Click "Add Friend" on someone's profile to send a friend request.

Privacy & Security

Go to Settings > Privacy to control who can see your posts, send you messages, and find your profile. You can customize these settings for different types of content and interactions.

Posting & Sharing

You can share text posts, photos, videos, and links. Make sure your content follows our Community Guidelines and doesn't violate copyright or contain inappropriate material.

Click the three-dot menu on your post and select "Delete". Note that deleted posts cannot be recovered, so make sure you really want to remove them.
